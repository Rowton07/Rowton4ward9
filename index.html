About Brandon

A neighbor who knows what’s at stake — and why Ward 9 deserves a school board member who listens.

I grew up in and around the same schools our students walk into every day. I’ve seen what happens when decisions are made without listening to the people in the classroom or at the kitchen table.

Ward 9 deserves a school board member who treats every policy, every budget line, it affects a real family — because it does.

What shapes my perspective:

  • Grounded in public education and real‑world experience.
  • Committed to listening to all the parties involved: parents, teachers, students, and staff.
  • Focused on creating a long-term plan and outlook for our school system

Priorities

Prioritizing school saftey and ensuring money isn't wasted fruitlessly.

Safe, Stable Classrooms

Reforming teacher and student guidelines and regulations. I want to ensure our policies have the input from all parties involved.

Transparent Budgets

Families deserve to know where every dollar goes. I'll work to ensure our ward knows where its money is going.

Support for Educators

I support reforming our school lunch program. Our students should feel comfortable eating the food they're provided.

Plan for Ward 9

A steady, transparent approach to leadership.

How I’ll approach the work

A school board seat isn’t a microphone — it’s a responsibility. My approach is simple: listen first, ask direct questions, and keep Ward 9 at the center of every decision.

1. Listen Before Voting

I’ll seek input from Ward 9 families, teachers, and staff — not just central office.

2. Ask Hard Questions About Spending

I’ll push for clear explanations of how money moves and how it affects classrooms.

3. Keep Communication Open

Families shouldn’t have to dig for basic information. I’ll advocate for clearer updates and better outreach.
