Budgets That Make Sense

Every dollar spent by LaSalle Parish schools belongs to the public. Ward 9 families deserve to know exactly where it goes.

Brandon's Position

The Full Plan

A school board member who doesn't ask hard questions about spending isn't doing their job. I will dig into every line item — not to obstruct, but to make sure Ward 9 gets its fair share and that our money is serving students, not bureaucracy.

Budget transparency isn't a partisan issue — it's a basic accountability issue. When spending decisions are made behind closed doors and reported in jargon-heavy documents that most parents can't parse, trust erodes and oversight fails.

Brandon will work to ensure that Ward 9 residents have access to plain-language budget summaries that clearly show how funds are allocated, where increases or cuts are happening, and what impact those decisions are expected to have on students.

Brandon will also push to ensure that Ward 9 schools receive equitable funding relative to other parts of the parish — and that any discrepancies are explained and addressed through the proper channels.

Specific Commitments

01

Plain-language budget summaries. Brandon will advocate for simplified, publicly available budget documents that any Ward 9 parent can understand — not just financial professionals.

02

Ask the hard questions. Before every budget vote, Brandon will push for detailed explanations of major expenditures and ensure the board understands what it's approving.

03

Track spending against outcomes. Money should produce results. Brandon will advocate for connecting budget decisions to measurable student outcomes so the community can evaluate whether spending is working.

04

Equitable distribution for Ward 9. Brandon will monitor ward-level allocations to ensure Ward 9 isn't shortchanged in the distribution of parish-wide resources.
